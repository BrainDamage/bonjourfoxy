You've just installed BonjourFoxy Beta

Firefox is now service discovery enabled but there's a few things you may want configure...

Toolbar Button

By default the Sidebar is accessed via the View→Sidebars menu. A toolbar button makes it a click away.

Service Indicator

See at a glance whether services are available — in the lower right-hand corner of the browser window a or will appear to indicate whether services are available.

Service Alerts

Get a visual notification when a new service is discovered.

Configuration options including the features above can be accessed by opening Add-ons via the Tools menu, selecting BonjourFoxy and then clicking PreferencesOptions. A generic Bonjour Browser can also be accessed by clicking 'Bonjour Browser' in the Tools menu.
